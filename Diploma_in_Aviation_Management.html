The Diploma in Aviation Management at NashVille Aviation is designed to prepare students for a variety of roles within the aviation industry, including airport management, airline operations, and regulatory compliance. Graduates of this program emerge with a well-rounded skill set and a deep understanding of the complexities and opportunities within the aviation sector. Elevate your career and take flight with a Diploma in Aviation Management.

  • Introduction to Aviation Industry
  • Ground Staff Services
  • Passenger Handling & Airport Management
  • Personality Development
  • Soft Skills
  • Grooming Sessions
  • Interview Training
  • Group Discussion and Extempore Training
  • Aptitude Test
  • Emergency Procedure Training
  • First Aid Training
  • Front Office Departmental Procedure
  • Food & Beverage Departmental Procedure
